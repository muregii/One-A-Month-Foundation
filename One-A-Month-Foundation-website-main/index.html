Information About US

One A Month foundation was founded in May 2021 with the aim of donating sanitary towels to needy, school-going girls, menstrual health education and mentorship. This is achieved by donations from well-wishers.

The foundation was founded due to the increasing need of providing school-going girls with enough sanitary towels keeping in mind that 3 out of 10 girls in Kenya can afford sanitary pads

It has an active membership comprising of youths, but is open to members of any age. It operates in Kenya and donations target marginalised rural areas and slum areas of Kenya's cities and towns.
Slogan - Help a girl, save a future.
